Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

MindBite ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

Information We Collect

MindBite is designed with privacy as a priority. We collect minimal data:

  • Local Data Only: Your preferences (selected categories, theme, notification time), bookmarks, reading history, and streak data are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your phone.
  • Anonymous Device ID: We generate a random identifier stored on your device to track your personal progress (streaks, bookmarks). This is not linked to your identity and is not shared with anyone.
  • No Account Required: MindBite does not require registration, login, email, or any personal information to use.

Third-Party Services

  • Unsplash: We use Unsplash to display photographs on knowledge cards. Unsplash may collect anonymous usage data per their privacy policy. Photo credits are displayed on every card.
  • Supabase: If connected, our backend service Supabase stores card content and anonymous bookmark data. No personal information is stored. See Supabase privacy policy.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • No location data
  • No contacts or address book data
  • No photos, camera, or microphone access
  • No browsing history outside the app
  • No advertising identifiers (until ads are implemented)

Data Storage

All user data (preferences, bookmarks, streaks, reading history) is stored locally on your device. If you uninstall MindBite, this data is permanently deleted. We do not back up your data to any cloud service unless you explicitly connect to our optional backend.

Children's Privacy

MindBite is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Advertising

MindBite currently does not display third-party advertisements. If ads are introduced in a future update, this privacy policy will be updated to reflect what data advertising partners may collect. You will be notified of any changes.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of MindBite after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
